My name is Sébastien Vandenitte. I am a postdoctoral researcher at the Sign Language Centre of the University of Jyväskylä, Finland.

I hold BA and MA degrees from Université Libre de Bruxelles and a PhD degree from the LSFB-Lab of the Université de Namur, Belgium.

Research interests

  • (Belgian) French
  • LSFB (French Belgian Sign Language)
  • comparative linguistics and semiotics
  • gesture and multimodality
  • depiction & iconicity
  • the building & use of comparable corpora
  • multi-method approaches to language as a phenomenon
